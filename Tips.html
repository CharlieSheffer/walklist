Tips for Doorbelling

charlie@charliesheffer.com for requests/bugs

Be Safe!

  • Don't walk on busy streets without a sidewalk, look up from this app when you walk.
  • Never go into anyone's home. Ever.
  • Skip houses where dogs are running free.
  • Record your car location on the app so you can find it again.

Before starting

  • Find a public place to park your car, in front of a hedge or fence is good too.
  • Practice. Run through what you will say at the door.
  • Plan a route that avoids you walking distances without doors. e.g. Loops are good, down one side of a street and back up another are good.

At the door

  • If their newspaper is on the driveway, take it up!
  • Press the doorbell firmly, but only once.
  • Step back from the door
  • Smile while you wait, you are being watched.
  • Be friendly and upbeat, it'll influence your tone and how the campaign is preceived.
  • If someone is curt, rude or shuts the door on you, end with "I am so sorry to have interrupted your day."
  • Never try to answer a question on an issue if you are not positive you know the answer. Tell them your candidate will mail them a response.
